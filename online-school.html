OUR MISSION

Why We Built Magnitude

Traditional coaching has failed millions of students. Rigid schedules, one-size-fits-all teaching, no teacher choice, and sky-high fees. We're fixing that.

Magnitude was born from a simple frustration: why do students have to travel 2 hours to a coaching center, sit in a batch of 100+ students, and learn from teachers they didn't even choose?

Our founders — all IIT Bombay, Delhi & Kanpur alumni — saw friends and juniors struggle not because they lacked talent, but because the coaching system was broken. Expensive, inflexible, and impersonal.

The Magnitude Difference

We built Magnitude on three core principles:

1. Student Choice: You pick your teachers. Try different educators, switch anytime, find the perfect match. Education is personal — your teacher should be too.

2. Complete Flexibility: Study from home, school, a café, anywhere. Attend live or watch recordings. Build your own schedule. No rigid 6 AM - 10 PM marathons.

3. Transparent & Affordable: At resaonable price, gets you everything — live classes, test series, AI doubt-solving, recordings. No ₹2L annual fees. Cancel anytime.

Our Impact

Since 2020, we've helped over 12,000 students across India (and UAE, Singapore, USA) prepare for JEE, NEET, and Boards. 2,800+ of them have cracked IIT or top medical colleges.

But beyond the numbers, what we're most proud of is this: students actually enjoy learning with us. They're not stressed, they're not burnt out — they're motivated, confident, and happy.
