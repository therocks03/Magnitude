HOW IT WORKS

Your Mentorship Journey

From onboarding to exam day — here's how we support you every step of the way.

Step 1: Diagnostic Assessment

We start with a comprehensive assessment to understand your current level, learning style, strengths, and improvement areas.

  • Subject-wise diagnostic test
  • Learning style analysis
  • Goal & timeline discussion
Step 2: Mentor Matching

You're paired with an IITian mentor who matches your needs, personality, and exam goals. Not happy? Switch anytime.

  • Meet your mentor on intro call
  • Review their background & expertise
  • Switch mentors if needed
Step 3: Custom Plan Creation

Your mentor creates a personalized study roadmap covering all subjects, topics, and timelines leading to your exam.

  • Chapter-wise breakdown
  • Weekly targets & milestones
  • Revision & test schedule
Step 4: Weekly Check-ins

Regular 1:1 sessions to review progress, solve doubts, adjust plans, and keep you on track. Your mentor is your accountability partner.

  • Progress review & feedback
  • Doubt clarification
  • Next week's action plan
